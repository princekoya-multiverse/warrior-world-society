The Origin

It started with a car accident

January 2021. A car accident changed everything. The doctors said Chelsy Koya would be lucky to stand — let alone walk.

Her husband Prince refused to accept that. He started filming everything. Every practitioner visit. Every workout. Every meal. Every protocol. He built a system of evidence because the system had no answers.

The community rallied. The City of New Westminster raised over $10,000 for Chelsy's recovery. A movement was born.

That movement became Warrior World. What started as one husband fighting for his wife is now a data-proven, AI-powered platform helping people with invisible disabilities across Canada.

This is not a testimonial. This is clinical proof on camera.

"If the system can't save the person you love most, you build your own system. So I did."

— Prince Koya, Founder

Chelsy is not a patient in our program. She is the reason the program exists. Her journey from "unlikely to stand" to active recovery is the living proof that cellular restoration works.

Today, she serves as a Director of the Warrior World Health & Performance Society — turning her personal fight into a scalable system for every warrior after her.
